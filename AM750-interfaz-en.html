Architecture and userflows

The site's information architecture was designed to remove duplicated content on the site. The objective was to simplify the information and avoid generating confusion among users. The multiple entries to the website were considered and specific userflows were designed in relation to different types of users.

Wireframes

In the first instance, two possible structures for the website were considered: The suggestion of the UX research report suggested showing more amount of content on the first scroll. That is why an option with two columns + an ad and another with three columns + ad was proposed

Stylesheet

Although developers participated and were consulted during throughout the design process, a stylesheet was created in order to facilitate the development of the components and, at the same time, communicate to the entire organization design criteria for future iterations.
